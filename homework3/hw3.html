personality
IQ is 187, and he has an eidetic memory. Judging by the anecdotal comments, he has an extensive supply of knowledge about a lot of things. Nevertheless, Sheldon is extremely weak in everything related to social intelligence. Sheldon has a number of qualities that are usually manifested in people with autism spectrum disorders, such as weak social activity, lack of empathy, anancastic personality disorder and many other features that make him one of the most eccentric and bizarre characters in the series, Sheldon's behavior is largely consistent with Syndrome Asperger. Features of his character include: Synesthesia. Sheldon intuitively senses prime numbers. Speaking of her favorite number, Sheldon explains: “73 is the 21st prime number. Written on the contrary, it is 37 - this is the 12th prime number, and if you write it on the contrary - 21, it is the result of multiplying seven and three, and also the number 73 written in the binary number system is a palindrome: 1-0-0-1-0 -0-1. " Anancastous personality disorder: Sheldon has a strict adherence to the schedule of his activities: eating, visiting the bathroom, performing his duties or doing laundry - all according to the schedule. Narcissism: Sheldon often feels the need for admiration for his own person and constantly indicates his intellectual superiority. Misophobia: worries if someone touches his food or himself, washes his hands as often as he can, and takes a shower twice a day. Hypochondria: Sheldon is often worried about getting sick. Inability to lie: When Sheldon becomes an accomplice in a lie, he tries to make every effort to make what is said believable, but often brings it to the point of absurdity. Unable to keep secrets.
Inability to sit in different places: Sheldon refuses to sit anywhere other than his own place on the couch in his apartment, which he considers “the only point of permanence in a changing world” and “a special place in the space around which the entire Universe revolves.” Intolerance of people in their bedroom. Pathological hoarding. Fear of the scene. Although Sheldon can quite easily speak with small groups, he cannot communicate with the crowd. Sheldon's phobias - dogs, birds and nets. Sheldon has some “childish” qualities. He, it would seem, does not understand this and considers this a way to achieve his goal. Sheldon drinks alcohol only in exceptional cases, and he tries not to drink caffeine and some other substances at all. Sheldon adheres to a strict diet: he eats certain foods on certain days (for example, “pizza evening”, “Thai food evening”), and his food has specific features, for example, a certain type of mustard, chopped broccoli instead of grated, a certain order of ingredients in a sandwich etc. Hobbies Like his fellow scientists, Sheldon loves comics, video games and science fiction. He is a fan of many films and TV shows (Star Cruiser Galaxy, Doctor Who, Star Gate, Star Wars, Star Trek), but he hates Babylon 5. Of course, Sheldon’s favorite character is Spock from the Star Trek series, because they have a lot in common: they are very smart, practically do not express their emotions and hate to break the rules. Sheldon has absolute pitch and can immediately play any melody on any instrument, with the exception of the violin. He plays on: piano (synthesizer); flute; theremin; bongo; tuba; bass guitar. In addition, she owns Tuvan throat singing. However, he never studied music purposefully, as he is convinced that all musicians are drug addicts. Sheldon has a “strike” system if someone violates his rules. If one person receives three “strikes”, he gives him a choice: either apologize or attend his lectures. One of Sheldon’s biggest dreams is a visit LHC (Large Hadron Collider).

work
Sheldon is a published theoretical physicist who has two doctoral degrees and a master's degree.
He conducts string theory research at the California Institute of Technology. Scientific degrees: “I’m Dr. Sheldon Cooper, BS, MS, MA, PhD, and ScD. ”, Which means: BS - Bachelor of Science, MS - Master of Science, MA - Master of Arts, PhD - Doctor of Philosophy and ScD - Doctor of Science. Sheldon does not consider geology to be a science, and also treats the humanities and biology with contempt.
